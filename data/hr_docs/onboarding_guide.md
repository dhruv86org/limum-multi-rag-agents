# New Employee Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to Your First Day!

Congratulations on joining TechCorp! This guide will help you navigate your first weeks and set you up for success.

## Pre-First Day Checklist

Before your start date, you should have:
- ✓ Completed all new hire paperwork (I-9, W-4, direct deposit)
- ✓ Selected health insurance and benefit elections
- ✓ Reviewed employee handbook
- ✓ Confirmed start date, time, and location with HR

## First Day Schedule

### Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
- 9:00 AM: Arrive at reception, check in
- 9:15 AM: Meet with HR for orientation
- 10:00 AM: IT setup (computer, email, accounts)
- 11:00 AM: Office tour and introductions
- 11:30 AM: Meet your manager and team

### Afternoon (1:00 PM - 5:00 PM)
- 1:00 PM: Lunch with your onboarding buddy
- 2:00 PM: Department overview and role expectations
- 3:00 PM: Review first week goals
- 4:00 PM: Set up workspace and tools
- 4:30 PM: Q&A with manager

## First Week Goals

### Week 1 Objectives
By the end of your first week, you should:
- Complete all HR and compliance training modules
- Set up your development environment and tools
- Schedule 1:1 meetings with immediate team members
- Understand team structure and communication norms
- Complete first small task or project

### Training Modules Required
All employees must complete:
1. Code of Conduct (30 minutes)
2. Information Security Basics (45 minutes)
3. Anti-Harassment Training (60 minutes)
4. Data Privacy and GDPR (45 minutes)
5. Workplace Safety (30 minutes)

Access training portal: https://learning.techcorp.com

## 30-60-90 Day Plan

### First 30 Days: Learn
- Absorb information about products, customers, and processes
- Build relationships with team and cross-functional partners
- Shadow experienced team members
- Complete assigned training and certifications
- Deliver first meaningful contribution

### Days 31-60: Contribute
- Take on increasing responsibility
- Begin working independently on tasks
- Participate actively in team meetings
- Identify improvement opportunities
- Seek feedback regularly

### Days 61-90: Impact
- Work independently on significant projects
- Demonstrate competency in core role responsibilities
- Contribute ideas for team improvement
- Begin mentoring newer team members
- Performance review with manager

## Important Policies Review

### Time Tracking
- Exempt employees: No time tracking required
- Non-exempt employees: Clock in/out via HR system
- Work hours submitted and approved weekly

### Expense Reimbursement
To get reimbursed for business expenses:
1. Submit expenses within 30 days via Expensify
2. Include itemized receipts
3. Add business purpose and attendees
4. Manager approval required for expenses over $100
5. Reimbursement processed in 5-7 business days

### Travel Policy
For business travel:
- Book through corporate travel portal
- Flights: Economy class for domestic, premium economy for international 6+ hours
- Hotels: Up to $200/night in major cities
- Meals: $75/day (receipts required over $25)
- Ground transportation: Uber/Lyft for business use

### Remote Work
After 90-day probation:
- Submit remote work request via HR portal
- Manager approval required
- Maximum 3 days per week (role dependent)
- Must be available during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM)
- Expected to attend office for important meetings

## Common Questions

### Q: When will I receive my first paycheck?
A: Payday is the 15th and last day of each month. Your first check depends on your start date.

### Q: How do I request time off?
A: Submit PTO requests through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance. Manager approval required.

### Q: What if I'm sick?
A: Notify your manager as soon as possible and log sick time in HR system. No documentation needed for 1-2 days.

### Q: Can I work from home?
A: After your 90-day probation period, you can request remote work subject to manager approval.

### Q: How do I access the office after hours?
A: Your badge provides access 6 AM - 8 PM. After-hours access requires security approval.

### Q: What if I have technical issues?
A: Contact IT helpdesk at helpdesk@techcorp.com or (555) 123-4569 for support.

### Q: When is open enrollment for benefits?
A: November 1-30 annually. New hires have 30 days from start date to enroll.

### Q: How do I change my direct deposit?
A: Log into HR portal and update banking information under "My Profile."
