# Employee Handbook

## Welcome to TechCorp

Welcome to TechCorp! This handbook contains essential information about our company policies, benefits, and workplace guidelines.

## Company Mission and Values

TechCorp is committed to innovation, collaboration, and employee well-being. Our core values include:
- Innovation: We constantly push boundaries and embrace new ideas
- Integrity: We operate with transparency and honesty
- Collaboration: We work together to achieve common goals
- Diversity: We celebrate differences and foster inclusion

## Employment Policies

### Working Hours
Standard working hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. However, we offer flexible working arrangements including:
- Flex-time: Start between 7:00 AM and 10:00 AM
- Remote work: Up to 3 days per week after probation period
- Compressed work weeks: Available with manager approval

### Attendance and Punctuality
Employees are expected to maintain regular attendance and arrive on time for scheduled shifts. If you need to be absent or late:
1. Notify your manager as soon as possible
2. Log absence in the HR system within 2 hours
3. Provide documentation for medical absences exceeding 3 days

### Dress Code
TechCorp maintains a business casual dress code:
- Monday-Thursday: Business casual attire
- Friday: Casual attire (jeans acceptable)
- Client meetings: Business professional attire required
- No offensive graphics or slogans on clothing

## Compensation and Benefits

### Salary Structure
TechCorp uses a competitive salary structure with annual reviews:
- Entry level (L1-L2): $60,000 - $85,000
- Mid level (L3-L4): $85,000 - $125,000
- Senior level (L5-L6): $125,000 - $175,000
- Leadership (L7+): $175,000+

Salaries are reviewed annually in January with potential adjustments based on performance and market conditions.

### Health Insurance
Full-time employees receive comprehensive health coverage:
- Medical: PPO and HMO plans available
- Dental: Full coverage for preventive care
- Vision: Annual eye exams and $200 frame allowance
- Coverage begins on first day of employment
- Company pays 80% of premium for employee
- Dependent coverage available at employee cost

### Retirement Plans
401(k) Retirement Savings Plan:
- Employees eligible after 90 days
- Company matches 50% up to 6% of salary
- Immediate vesting on employee contributions
- Company contributions vest over 4 years (25% per year)
- Multiple investment options available

### Paid Time Off (PTO)
TechCorp offers generous PTO that increases with tenure:
- Years 0-2: 15 days per year
- Years 3-5: 20 days per year
- Years 6+: 25 days per year
- PTO accrues monthly
- Maximum carryover: 40 hours to next year
- Unused PTO over limit is paid out at year-end

### Holidays
TechCorp observes 11 paid holidays annually:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (2 days)
- Christmas Eve
- Christmas Day
- New Year's Eve

### Sick Leave
Separate from PTO, employees receive:
- 10 sick days per year
- Does not accrue or carry over
- Can be used for personal illness or family care
- No medical documentation required for 1-2 days
- Extended illness requires doctor's note

### Parental Leave
New parents receive comprehensive leave:
- Birth mothers: 16 weeks paid leave
- Non-birth parents: 8 weeks paid leave
- Adoption: 8 weeks paid leave
- Leave can be taken within 12 months of birth/adoption
- Job protection guaranteed during leave

## Professional Development

### Training and Education
TechCorp invests in employee growth:
- Annual learning budget: $2,000 per employee
- Can be used for courses, certifications, conferences
- Manager approval required
- Reimbursement within 30 days of completion

### Tuition Reimbursement
For degree programs related to your role:
- Up to $5,000 per year
- Must maintain B grade or higher
- 2-year employment commitment after completion
- Pre-approval required from HR

### Career Advancement
Internal mobility is encouraged:
- Job postings shared internally before external posting
- Employees can interview after 1 year in current role
- Lateral moves supported for skill development
- Annual performance reviews include development planning

## Workplace Policies

### Anti-Discrimination Policy
TechCorp prohibits discrimination based on:
- Race, color, national origin
- Gender, gender identity, sexual orientation
- Religion or creed
- Age (40 and older)
- Disability status
- Veteran status
- Pregnancy or parental status

Violations should be reported to HR immediately.

### Harassment Policy
We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for harassment:
- Sexual harassment of any kind is prohibited
- Bullying, intimidation, or hostile behavior not tolerated
- Retaliation against complainants is strictly forbidden
- All complaints investigated promptly and confidentially

### Drug and Alcohol Policy
To maintain a safe workplace:
- Illegal drugs prohibited on company property
- Alcohol only permitted at company-approved events
- Employees cannot work while impaired
- Random drug testing may be conducted for safety-sensitive positions

### Social Media Policy
Guidelines for social media use:
- Personal accounts must not claim to represent company
- Confidential information cannot be shared
- Respectful communication expected
- Work time should not be spent on personal social media

### Confidentiality and Data Security
Employees must protect company information:
- Customer data must be handled according to privacy policies
- Proprietary information cannot be shared externally
- Clean desk policy: secure documents when away
- Data breaches must be reported immediately

## Performance Management

### Performance Reviews
Annual performance review process:
- Self-assessment completed in December
- Manager review in January
- Performance ratings: Exceptional, Exceeds, Meets, Needs Improvement
- Ratings determine salary adjustments and bonuses

### Performance Improvement Plans (PIP)
For underperforming employees:
- 90-day structured improvement plan
- Clear goals and regular check-ins
- Support resources provided
- Successful completion returns to normal status
- Failure may result in termination

### Recognition Programs
We celebrate employee achievements:
- Monthly MVP awards ($500 bonus)
- Peer recognition platform
- Annual awards ceremony
- Spot bonuses for exceptional work

## Termination and Resignation

### Voluntary Resignation
If you choose to leave TechCorp:
- Provide 2 weeks written notice (4 weeks for managers)
- Complete exit interview with HR
- Return all company property
- Final paycheck includes unused PTO

### Involuntary Termination
Termination may occur for:
- Performance issues after PIP
- Violation of company policies
- Misconduct or ethical violations
- Position elimination (layoff)

Severance packages available for layoffs based on tenure.

### Final Pay
Upon termination:
- Final paycheck issued on last day or next pay period
- Unused PTO paid out
- Benefits continue through end of month
- COBRA information provided for insurance continuation

## Workplace Safety

### Emergency Procedures
In case of emergency:
- Fire: Evacuate via nearest exit, gather at designated assembly point
- Medical: Call 911, notify reception for on-site first aid
- Security threat: Lock doors, hide, contact authorities

### Workplace Injuries
If injured at work:
- Report immediately to manager and HR
- Seek medical attention if needed
- Complete incident report within 24 hours
- Workers' compensation available for work-related injuries

### Ergonomics
To prevent repetitive strain injuries:
- Ergonomic assessments available upon request
- Sit-stand desks provided for those who need them
- Regular breaks encouraged
- Proper computer workstation setup required

## Technology and Equipment

### Company Equipment
Employees receive:
- Laptop or desktop computer
- Monitor(s) as needed for role
- Phone stipend: $75/month for remote workers
- Software licenses as required

All equipment remains company property and must be returned upon termination.

### IT Security
Required security practices:
- Strong passwords (12+ characters, changed every 90 days)
- Multi-factor authentication on all systems
- VPN required for remote access
- Suspicious emails must be reported to IT

### Personal Device Use
BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) policy:
- Personal devices can access email if MDM installed
- Company reserves right to wipe company data
- Personal use of company devices acceptable within reason
- No illegal downloads or streaming
